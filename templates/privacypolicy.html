What information does Oceanic Express capture about visitors to its web site? The Oceanic Express.com web server uses an extended log file format which captures: date and time of visit, referring address (location from which a visitor comes to Oceanic Express.com), type of Internet browser, and visitor's IP address (Each computer that connects to the Internet is assigned a unique number, an IP address, for identification purposes). The log file does not capture a visitor's email address. 2. Does Oceanic Express ask for personal information? Several areas of Oceanic Express.com ask for personal information. They include: Online Account Registration, features of Manage My Account, and registration pages for Oceanic Express Ship Manager at Oceanic Express.com, Oceanic Express Ship Manager Lite, Oceanic Express Global Trade Manager, Oceanic Express InSight and My Oceanic Express. This list may be expanded without notice. In the event that it is expanded, additional information may be required. In these areas, your name, address, email address, billing information, and business profile are requested. This information is collected to help us further develop our services, to provide you access to valuable Oceanic Express Internet-based information and services, and to bill you for those services if you elect to use them. 3. We also use this information to send you email notifications about updates to the web site, and to contact you by other means for marketing and other purposes. Our policy is to not give, sell or otherwise distribute the information collected through this site to third parties outside of Oceanic Express Corporation and its subsidiaries (unless required by law); provided, however, in some cases we may use suppliers to assist us in collecting, using or otherwise processing for our benefit the information obtained through this site. What happens when I leave Oceanic Express.com to visit a Oceanic Express linked web site? Parts of Oceanic Express.com, such as Oceanic Express Global Trade Manager, consist of products and services hosted by third-party businesses. When you leave Oceanic Express.com to visit one of these sites, the only information transferred to the third-party is the fact that you came from Oceanic Express.com (the referring address). This practice allows the third-party to monitor its own web site traffic, but does not provide them with any information about you. Oceanic Express is not responsible or liable for the independent privacy policies of our third-party sites. You should consult the privacy policies at those sites to determine how your information may be used. 5. What is a cookie? A cookie is a series of data characters that, when programmed into a web site, is placed by the web server into the browser's application folder on your computer. Once placed onto your machine, the cookie will allow the web site to "recognize" you as a unique individual. 6. Does Oceanic Express use cookies? Cookies are used to achieve two goals. The first is to provide Oceanic Express with the capability to personalize information for certain segments of its customer base. Secondly, in some instances, cookies are used to allow Oceanic Express the opportunity to associate individual customers with their information profiles. For example, through the use of cookies, Oceanic Express.com Login can "remember" your user ID when you login to Oceanic Express Ship Manager at Oceanic Express.com on your computer. 7. Can cookies be removed from my hard drive? Yes, cookies can be removed from your hard drive. Also, depending on what type of web browser and what browser version you are using, you may be able to change the properties on your cookie file so that cookies are not used or saved. Please check with your browser provider for more information on removing cookies. 8. How do I contact Oceanic Express? Our Corporate postal address is: Federal Express European Services Inc.
